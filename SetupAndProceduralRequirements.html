1. Setup And Procedural Requirements

There are two facets for the daily trasmits, configuration and procedure.

Configuration is an initial one time setup, with possible operations necessitated by changes to your business flow. Configuration involves setting up and output directory, creating GL Codes, and assigning GL Codes to Sub-Categories and Payment methods.

Procedurally there are two main functions to perform to generate files, as well as a Monthly Process. The daily process is to first do all shift reconciliations for the day, then run the day end process for the day.

Although not strictly necessary for daily transmit files, there is an additional process after a month is complete. When running the day end process you will also generate Business Date Reports, and any with monthly or yearly totals are dependent on the Month End Process being run when a month is finished.
